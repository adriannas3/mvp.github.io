Signs of Domestic Abuse:

In case you may be unsure, we're here to provide examples of possible signs of domestic violence that may or may not be apparent. Abuse can come in any form, financially, physically, emotionally or sexually. If any of these signs are present, please access the resources provided throughout our site to get help and/or support.

    Emotional Abuse

  • Extreme Jealousy
  • Embarrasses you in front of others, and it makes you want to avoid people
  • Yells at you and makes you feel small
  • Criticizes you

    • Sexual Abuse

  • Pressuring you to perform sexual acts that you're not comfortable with.
  • Tries to give you an STD
  • Makes you dress in a sexual way
